Dr.APJ Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India , The people's President

Here's a timeline of his Life..

  • 1931 - Born in Rameshwaram, Tamil Nadu.
  • 1954 - Graduated in physics from University of madras.
  • 1960 - Graduated in Aerospace Engineering from Madras Institute of Techonology And Joined Defence Research and Development Organisation as a scientist.
  • 1969 - Joined Space Research at ISRO.
  • 1980 - Became Chief Executive of Integrated Guided Missile Development Programme, Played major part in developing many missiles for India like Agni, Prithvi.
  • 1981 - Awarded Padma Bhushan by Government of India
  • 1990 - Awarded Padma Vibhushan by Government of India
  • 1992 - Served as the Chief Scientific Adviser to the Prime Minister and Secretary of the Defense Research and Development Organisation.
  • 1997 - Awarded Bharat Ratna by President of India and Indira Gandhi award for National Integration by Indian National Congress.
  • 2002 - Became 11th President of India.
  • 2012 - Launched a programme for the youth of India called the What Can I Give Movement, with a central theme of defeating corruption.
  • 2015 - Passes away at the age of 83 while doing what he loved the most,addressing students.
